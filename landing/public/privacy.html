Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

We hate dark patterns and we hate boilerplate. This page tells you exactly what we know about you, what we do with it, and what you can ask us to stop. Where we say "we", we mean Codetrail (operated by Trace, contact at codetrail@dipgle.com).

1. Local-first by default

If you only use the free tier of Codetrail (hooks, template, browser viewer), no data leaves your machine, ever. The viewer parses SQLite files entirely in your browser via WASM — there is no upload, no server round-trip. We literally cannot see your devlog.

2. What we collect on this website

  • Email addresses you submit via the waitlist form, used only to email you when beta access opens. Stored until you ask us to delete or beta access has been delivered + 90 days.
  • Standard server logs (IP, user agent, requested path) retained 30 days for abuse prevention. Not used for advertising or product analytics.
  • No third-party analytics or trackers (no Google Analytics, no Facebook pixel, no fingerprinting).

3. Paid tiers (Pro, Team)

When you sync devlogs to the cloud (Pro/Team feature), we store the SQLite contents encrypted at rest, scoped to your account. We never train models on your devlog, never share with third parties, never look at it except when you grant explicit support access. Retention: live for as long as your account is active; deleted within 30 days of account closure or explicit delete request.

4. Sub-processors

We use a small number of vendors to operate Codetrail. Each has signed a Data Processing Agreement and is bound by GDPR-equivalent terms. Current list:

  • tfl5 (Hanoi, Vietnam) — hosting + database + storage for the cloud sync feature.
  • Stripe (USA / Ireland) — credit-card and international payments. We never see your card details.
  • VietQR / MoMo / domestic banks (Vietnam) — Vietnamese-currency payments. Same isolation.
  • Google Workspace (USA / EU) — operational email (the mailbox you write to). Subject to Google's standard contractual clauses.

We will update this list at least 30 days before adding a new sub-processor that handles personal data. You can object by email; if we can't accommodate, you can cancel your plan and we'll refund any unused prepaid period.

5. Security

Cloud-synced data is encrypted at rest (AES-256, vendor-managed keys) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Production access requires SSO + hardware key. Internal access to live data is restricted, logged, and used only for the support case you explicitly opened. We perform security review on dependencies before major releases. We're not SOC 2 certified yet — it's on the Team-tier roadmap.

6. International data transfers

Codetrail's primary hosting is in Vietnam. If you are in the EU/EEA, UK, or another jurisdiction that restricts international transfers, we rely on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (2021/914) and, where required, supplementary measures. Email us for the full transfer impact assessment.

7. Children's privacy

Codetrail is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a minor has provided us data, email us and we will delete it.

8. Data breach notification

If we discover a breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of discovery, per GDPR Art. 33–34. The notification will describe what happened, what data was affected, the likely consequences, and what we're doing about it.

9. Cookies

A single session cookie when you log in (Pro/Team). No tracking cookies. No advertising cookies. No "we use cookies, click OK" theater.

10. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA / Vietnamese PDPL)

Our lawful basis for processing your email (waitlist) is your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)); for paid-tier syncs it is contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)); for security logs it is legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)). You have the right to: access a copy of your data, rectify it, erase it, restrict processing, port it elsewhere, and object. CCPA users additionally have the right to opt out of any sale or share (we do neither). Email codetrail@dipgle.com and we will respond as soon as we reasonably can, and at the latest within the period required by applicable law. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (in Vietnam: the Ministry of Public Security under Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP).

11. Changes to this policy

Material changes get 30 days' email notice. The current version date is at the top.
